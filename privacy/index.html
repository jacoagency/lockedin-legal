Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Locked In ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Locked In mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and username when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Display name and profile picture you choose to provide.
  • Videos: Dance practice videos you record and upload within the app.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including practice sessions and progress.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To track your dance practice progress and achievements
  • To enable video recording and playback features
  • To send important updates about the app

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud platform. Videos are stored in secure cloud storage and are only accessible by you unless you choose to share them.

Camera and Photo Library Access

Locked In requires access to your camera to record practice videos and your photo library to save and select videos. We only access these features when you actively use them, and we do not access your camera or photos in the background.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time through the app settings, which will permanently delete all your data including videos, profile information, and practice history.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data upon request

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@lockedin.app
